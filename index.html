About Innovista

Innovista is an 1st edition of APEC ACM Student Chapter for IT hosted by Adhiparasakthi Engineering College, Melmaruvathur. Innovista is a Intra Departmental Student Competition and Technical Workshop igniting ideas who are making an impact today and inspiring tomorrow. It was started with an ideology to support and motivate the students in the field of IT. It aims at providing students to develop innovative practical solutions to pressing real-world problems.

About APEC

Adhiparasakthi Engineering College, situated in Melmaruvathur, Tamil Nadu, is a dedicated institution committed to nurturing the next generation of engineers with a unique blend of values and technological prowess. Since its inception, this college has been steadfast in its mission to cultivate engineers who not only excel in their chosen fields but also possess a deep sense of service and spirituality. Established with the vision of advancing the growth of society at large, Adhiparasakthi Engineering College places a strong emphasis on the adoption of cutting-edge technologies, ensuring their sustainability and relevance in an ever-evolving world. The institution's unwavering dedication to academic excellence and holistic development sets it apart in the educational landscape. https://apec.edu.in/
